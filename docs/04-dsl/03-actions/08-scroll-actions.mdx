<details id="i-scroll-to-the-top-or-bottom-of-page">
  <summary class="dsl">

  ```gherkin
  I scroll to the <top|bottom> of the page
  ```
  <p>Scrolls to the top or bottom of the current page.</p>
  </summary>
  <p>Where</p>
  <ul>
    <li>
      <code>&lt;top|bottom&gt;</code> is one of:
      <ul>
        <li><code>top</code> to scroll to the top of the page</li>
        <li><code>bottom</code> to scroll to the bottom of the page</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
  </ul>
  <p>Example</p>

  ```gherkin {2}
    Given I navigate to "https://automationintesting.com/selenium/testpage"
      And I scroll to the bottom of the page
  ```

<details id="i-scroll-to-the-top-or-bottom-of-element">
  <summary class="dsl">

  ```gherkin
  I scroll to the <top|bottom> of <element>
  ```
  <p>Scrolls to the top or bottom of the given element.</p>
  </summary>
  <p>Where</p>
  <ul>
    <li>
      <code>&lt;top|bottom&gt;</code> is one of:
      <ul>
        <li><code>top</code> to scroll to the top of the element</li>
        <li><code>bottom</code> to scroll to the bottom of the element</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li><code>&lt;element&gt;</code> the name of the element to scroll to</li>
  </ul>
  <p>Example</p>

  ```gherkin {3}
    Given the surname field can be located by id "surname"
     When I navigate to "https://automationintesting.com/selenium/testpage"
      And I scroll to the top of the surname field
  ```
